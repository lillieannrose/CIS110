The Downstairs Attic

Our Philosophy

We get it. Every bar attempts to have a clever name and a clever theme. The Downstairs Attic is just a fancy way of saying that we're a bar in a basement. But do not fret! It is quite spacious and quite air conditioned.

What We Offer

You handle the partying... we'll handle everything else. Our shelves are stocked from top to bottom with the best quality drinks a bar can offer, along with an assortment of snacks to keep that blood sugar up while dancing!
